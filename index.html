Shridev Pathshala is a dynamic and innovative English medium premier institution of Bhiwani. The imposing building is spread over 4 acres consisting of an Administrative Block, a Junior wing for primary classes, a Middle wing for secondary classes and a Senior wing for higher classes besides a vast play ground in the heart of the city.

It has grown into a full fledged Senior Secondary school affiliated to CBSE, New Delhi.
